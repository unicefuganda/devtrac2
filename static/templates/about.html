DevTracGlobal is a UNICEF project to visualize multi-themed data from multiple Open platform sources using a Dashboard.

All indicator data is available from DevInfo (http://www.devinfo.org/) and being organized for easier access through www.data.ug

The Site reports, Organizations and Activities (projects) are made available from www.devtrac.ug whose development work is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License

DevTracGlobal is still a work in progress and is currently at the proof-of-concept stage.
